Stock Correlation Project

End-to-end data pipeline that fetches 5 years of daily OHLCV prices for tech stocks, computes pairwise Pearson correlations, runs cointegration tests, generates rolling pairs-trading signals, persists results in PostgreSQL, surfaces them via an 8-tab Streamlit dashboard, and generates AI-powered regime commentary via Claude.

Purpose of the Analysis
  • Identify correlation patterns in strongly correlated stocks and their potential implications in pair trading strategies
  • Test pairs for cointegration (ADF + Engle-Granger) to confirm a stable long-run spread exists
  • Generate actionable LONG/SHORT/EXIT signals via rolling z-score and daily PnL tracking
  • Identify potential regime changes versus temporary market effects driving co-movement
